How A Cupcake Looks

  • They have round tops, a wrapper on the bottom and it is usually decorated. Some cupcakes have topping such as sprinkles and a cherry.

    • Different Flavors

  • Cupcakes have many flavors such as red velvet, cookies and cream, penut butter, chocolate and vanilla.

    • Different icing.

  • The icings may vary in different flavors such as peanut butter, chocolate,royal whipped creame, and butter creame.

    • How they Taste

  • Some are very sweet, some are soft, some are very moist, some are very appetizing and they are all delicous!
