  • Magic mango

    There once was a magic mango. Everyone who ate the mango would learn how to fly. For this reason, the mango was in danger. The mango was lonely and wanted to find its family.

    The mango would travel by hiding with normal mangoes. Eventually, the mango reached Magic Mountain. There, it found a river of magic juice and hundreds of trees filled with magic mangoes. The only animals in the area were bears allergic to mangoes.

    The mango had never been happier and lived safely on Magic Mountin for the rest of its life.
