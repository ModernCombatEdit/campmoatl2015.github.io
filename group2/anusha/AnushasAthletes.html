The Battle of Kryptish

Bobbi Dil

The biggest villan in Kryptish history, who fought the legendary Altharash.

She was a normal girl who was from New Dehli, India. It all started when she didn't get to play sports with the kids at school. After that year, a sense of hatred grew throughout her body. Nowadays, she is the average CRIMINAL until she took a wild chance.

Altharash

A heroic crime fighter who saves the city of Kryptish on a day to day basis.

He was the average naughty boy from Islamabad, Pakistan. He would always do mean things and insult people. But it all changed the day he saved a civilian from a moving train. After that day, he dedicated his life to keep the city safe, and he became ALTHARASH!!...and everything was perfect until Bobbi Dil showed up.
